Print your own copies of AI Must Die!

  1. Download the typeset PDF file
  2. Print, fold & staple
  3. Give them away!


Note: The file above is intended for duplex (double sided) printing. Depending on your printer, you may also need to set it to 'top binding' or 'on short edge' mode. If needed, you can also tweak the typeset file by uploading this version to Bookbinder JS.
